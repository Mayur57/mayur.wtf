Countless is a free and open-source Chrome extension that is designed to hide the view count on tweets across the entire Twitter platform.

With Countless, you can enjoy a cleaner and more streamlined Twitter experience without the distraction of constantly checking how many people have viewed your tweets. This extension is ideal for users who prefer not to see the annoying metric.

Countless is a lightweight extension that is easy to use and install. Simply install the extension on your Chromium-based browser, such as Google Chrome, Brave, Microsoft Edge, or any other browser that supports Chrome extensions. Once installed, Countless will automatically hide the view count on all tweets across the Twitter platform without needing you to enable it.

The extension is written using Javascript and is designed to be as unobtrusive as possible. It does not interfere with any other aspects of the Twitter platform, and it does not collect any personal data or user information. This ensures that your privacy is protected at all times.

Countless is a simple and effective Chrome extension that allows you to remove the view counts on your tweets. It's free, open-source, and easy to install, and it can help you focus on creating great content without the distraction of metrics. Give it a try today and see how it can enhance your Twitter experience!
